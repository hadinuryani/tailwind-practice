Benefits of consuming fresh fruit

Cavendish Banana

Cavendish bananas have a soft texture and thin skin. Some of the ingredients that can be found in cavendish bananas are vitamin A, vitamin C, the mineral phosphorus, calcium, potassium and carbohydrates. One of the benefits of consuming cavendish bananas is that they can prevent allergies and shortness of breath.

Shine
Muscat Grapes

This wine is characterized by a juicy flesh texture, a sweet taste similar to honey, and a refreshing aroma. The crunchy skin and lack of seeds make it very convenient to eat directly.The large size of the fruit and the fragrant smell make the shine muscat grape often referred to as the king of all types of grapes. Apart from being eaten directly, these grapes are also often processed into dessert wine.
